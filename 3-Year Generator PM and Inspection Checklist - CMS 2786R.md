## 4-Hour Load Test — NFPA 110 §8.4.2

> **Test Requirement:** Generator must be exercised under load conditions for **4 continuous hours**.  
> Test must include a **complete simulated cold start** and **automatic or manual transfer of all EES loads**.  
> Load during test must meet the criteria of NFPA 110 (typically ≥30% of nameplate rating unless load bank is used).

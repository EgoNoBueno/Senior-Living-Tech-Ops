## 1. Commercial Cooking Hood and Duct Cleaning (K324) ⭐ *REQUIRED SEMI-ANNUALLY*

> Reference: 18.3.2.5.1, 19.3.2.5.1 (LSC) | NFPA 96, *Standard for Ventilation Control and Fire Protection of Commercial Cooking Operations*  
> **NFPA 96 requires hood, plenum, and exhaust duct cleaning at least every 6 months** for most healthcare cooking operations. High-volume or solid-fuel cooking may require quarterly or monthly cleaning.
